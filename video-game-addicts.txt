1. What activity did you choose to examine?

We chose to examine Video game addiction and a way to stop it.

2. How did you examine it, with what methods?

We interviewed 3 willing participants in different ages ranging from child to adult who are addicted to video games. We 
also observed them while they are playing video games 

3. What major insights or breakdowns did you discover?

The user does not know when to stop playing video games. And  they also lose track of time. A major insight is that they
isolate themselves from others in order to spend more time gaming.

4. What promising unmet needs were identified as a result?

The participants need to not be a perfectionist. The reason why our users are addicted to video games is because they seek the need to complete everything 
right away or/and be the best.
